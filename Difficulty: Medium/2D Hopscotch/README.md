<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/hopscotch4857/1?page=1&category=CPP&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions">2D Hopscotch</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Aakriti, Avantika and Mehak are playing 2D Hopscotch. The arena is in the form of a <strong>n*m</strong> 2D matrix <strong>mat[]</strong>. But the position of the cells is slightly modified as shown below.&nbsp;</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><img src="https://contribute.geeksforgeeks.org/wp-content/uploads/hopscotch-1.jpg" alt=""></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Mehak starts the game from tile <strong>(i, j)</strong> while Avantika and Aakriti direct her. In each turn Mehak will collect all the stones present (1 or 2) steps away from where she is standing. Avantika can direct Mehak to take 1 step and and Aakriti can direct Mehak to take 2 steps.&nbsp; </span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">If the director <strong>d</strong> is known to you as d = 0 being Avantika and 1 being Aakriti, find the number of stones that Mehak will collect.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input:</strong> n = 3, m = 3, mat[][] = [[5, 9, 7], [6, 4, 5], [8, 1, 2]], d = 0, i = 1, j = 1
<strong>Output:</strong> 31
<strong>Explaination: </strong>Avantika is the director as d = 0, so Mehak can move 1 steps from (1,1) and from tiles at (0,1), (1,0), (1,2), (2,1), (2,2) and (2,0), she can collect 9 + 6 + 5 + 8 + 1 + 2 = 31 stones.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input:</strong> n = 3, m = 3, mat[][] = [[5, 9, 7], [6, 4, 5], [8, 1, 2]], d = 1, i = 1, j = 1
<strong>Output:</strong> 12
<strong>Explaination:</strong> Aakriti is the director as d = 1, so Mehak can move 2 steps from (1,1) and from tiles at (0,0) and (0,2), she can collect 5 + 7 = 12 stones.<br></span></pre>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Constraints:</strong></span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;">1 ≤ n, m ≤ 1000</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;">0 ≤ i &lt; n</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;">0 ≤ j &lt; m<br>1&nbsp;≤ mat[i][j]&nbsp;≤&nbsp;1000<br>0&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">≤ </span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">d&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">≤ 1</span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Matrix</code>&nbsp;<code>CPP</code>&nbsp;<code>Data Structures</code>&nbsp;
