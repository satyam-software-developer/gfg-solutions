<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/sum-of-all-sub-sequences-of-a-number3437/1?page=1&category=CPP&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions">Sum of All Digit Subsequences</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Basic</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Given a string<strong> s</strong> consisting of digits, find the <strong>sum</strong> of all digits present in every non-empty subsequence of <strong>s</strong>.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>s = "123" 
<strong>Output:</strong> 24
<strong>Explanation:</strong> The non-empty subsequences are: [1], [2], [3], [1, 2], [1, 3], [2, 3], [1, 2, 3]. <br>The sum of all digits in these subsequences is 24.
</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input:</strong> s = "405"
<strong>Output:</strong> 36
<strong>Explanation:</strong> The non-empty subsequences are: [4], [0], [5], [4, 0], [4, 5], [0, 5], [4, 0, 5]. <br>The sum of all digits in these subsequences is 36.
</span></pre>
